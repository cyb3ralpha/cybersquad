ABOUT CYBERSQUAD

A community of cybersecurity enthusiasts dedicated to learning and collaboration

Who We Are

Cybersquad is a community of passionate individuals who are interested in cybersecurity, ethical hacking, and information security. Our mission is to educate, collaborate, and empower members to build their skills and contribute to the cybersecurity field.

What We Do

  • Organize workshops, webinars, and hackathons
  • Provide learning resources and tutorials
  • Collaborate on projects and challenges
  • Promote awareness about cybersecurity best practices
  • Support members in building careers in cybersecurity

Our Mission

To create a vibrant community where cybersecurity enthusiasts can learn, grow, and make an impact while fostering ethical practices in technology.
